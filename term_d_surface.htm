d_surface (m)

Variable in the module Quinton.  It is the depth (m) from the soil surface to the middle of the water table and is equal to the depth of the frost table less half the sum of the layerwater of all layers above the frost table or (dmelt - Depth/2).  This depth determines the soil tension applied to the layers above.  It is also the depth from the surface used to select the physical properties such as the horizontal hydraulic conductivity.

Notes

    Depths are always handled as positive numbers.
